## Instructions

1. Add the cottage cheese, feta, milk, olives, dried mint, and pepper to a mixer or food processor.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Transfer the spread to a serving bowl.
4. Sprinkle with parsley or coriander.
5. Drizzle with olive oil before serving.

## Notes

A very simple spread that works well with fresh bread, toasted bread, crackers, or as part of a small mezze-style table.
