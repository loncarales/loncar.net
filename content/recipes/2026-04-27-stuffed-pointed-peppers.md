## Instructions

1. Finely chop the onion, garlic, parsley, and mint.
2. Add the chopped ingredients to a bowl with the ground beef, cottage cheese or quark, feta, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, cumin, and olive oil.
3. Knead everything together until the filling is well combined.
4. Fill the pointed peppers with the meat mixture. A piping bag makes this much easier, especially with narrow peppers.

### Oven method

1. Preheat the oven to 220 °C.
2. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish.
3. Bake for about 25 minutes, until the peppers are soft and the filling is cooked through.

### BBQ method

1. Grill the stuffed peppers on all sides for about 2–3 minutes to get some color.
2. Transfer them to a grill pan.
3. Grill for another 10–12 minutes, until the filling is cooked through.
4. Serve warm.

## Notes

Stuffed peppers are one of those dishes that appear in many cultures, each with its own version and filling. This one leans Mediterranean, with fresh herbs, feta, cumin, and a bit of heat from cayenne pepper.

It works beautifully in the oven, but if you have the BBQ going, the grill adds an extra layer of flavor.
