## Instructions

1. Crumble the cheese into a bowl.
2. Add the eggs, sour milk, oil, baking powder, flour, and a little salt.
3. Mix everything into a soft batter or dough.
4. Grease a baking tray well and spread the mixture into it. (You can also use baking paper if you prefer.)
5. Bake in a preheated oven until the pogača is golden and cooked through.
6. Serve warm, ideally with sour sheep milk if you have it.

## Notes

The original recipe uses sour milk and baking powder, but I sometimes make it with **kefir** instead of sour milk. Another variation I have tried is replacing the baking powder with **100 g of sourdough starter**, which gives it a slightly different character.

It is a humble but comforting bake, simple ingredients, old-school method, and best enjoyed warm.
