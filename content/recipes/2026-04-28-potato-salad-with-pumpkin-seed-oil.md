## Instructions

1. Cook the potatoes in their skins until tender.
2. Let the potatoes cool slightly, then peel them.
3. Slice the potatoes into rounds and place them in a bowl.
4. Thinly slice the onions and add them to the potatoes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Add a generous amount of pumpkin seed oil.
7. Dress with apple cider vinegar and gently mix everything together.
8. Let the salad rest for a little while before serving, so the potatoes can absorb the dressing.
9. Optionally, add cooked beans, although they are usually not part of the classic version.

## Notes

If you ask a random housewife for advice on potato salad, you might get three strict rules: only potatoes and onion, nothing else; prepare the marinade by reducing broth; and, for heaven’s sake, never use any oil other than sunflower oil, even olive oil is already too strong. 😂

In Prekmurje, we do not pay too much attention to that.

This version contains plenty of pumpkin seed oil, which brings a deep, nutty flavor, and the dressing is simple, no broth, no cooking, no complications.

In the Prekmurje dialect, this dish is called **Krumšova šalata s tikvinim oljon**, meaning potato salad with pumpkin seed oil.

As a bonus, you can add beans, but in the classic krumšova šalata they are usually not included.
