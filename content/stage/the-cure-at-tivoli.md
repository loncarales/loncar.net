# The Cure at Tivoli

The Cure at Tivoli is a collection of photographs documenting a memorable concert experience in Ljubljana, Slovenia. The show took place on June 14, 2001, at the iconic Tivoli venue, where the band The Cure performed to a packed audience. This set of photographs captures the energy and atmosphere of the night, showcasing the band's iconic stage presence and the vibrant crowd.

The concert was captured using a Nikon F90X camera with Kodak Tri-X 400 film, resulting in sharp and vibrant images that capture the essence of the performance. The photographs are a testament to the band's enduring legacy and the power of live music to bring people together.
